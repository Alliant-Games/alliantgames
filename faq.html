FAQ

I NEED ASSISTANCE FROM THE ALLIANT GAMES STAFF, WHERE DO I GO?

There are several ways to get help: Open a ticket in our Discord, use the chat widget on our website, or email us at support@alliant.gg

I'VE BEEN BANNED FROM THE SERVER, HOW DO I REQUEST AN UNBAN?

You can open a ban appeal ticket in our Discord. If you have been banned from the Discord, you can email us at support@alliant.gg or use our chat widget for assistance.

I THINK THE SERVER IS DOWN, HOW DO I CONFIRM?

Visit our Status Page at https://status.alliant.gg for the latest server status.

HOW DO I VERIFY MY ACCOUNT?

Visit our Verification Page at https://verify.alliant.gg to login with Steam and Discord to verify your accounts.

WHERE CAN I GET VIP AND PERKS?

Visit our Perks Shop at https://perks.alliant.gg to purchase VIP or additional Perks.

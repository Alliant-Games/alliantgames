ABOUT US

Alliant Games is a premier gaming community and server provider, dedicated to creating memorable, fun, and reliable experiences for players around the world.

Founded on July 24, 2020, Alliant Games operates as a proud subsidiary of Dusty Hansen Productions LLC. The company is headquartered in the United States at 2501 Chatham Rd STE N, Springfield, Illinois, and is established under the laws of the State of Illinois.

From its inception, Alliant Games has focused on fostering a strong alliance between players, developers, and staff—building a welcoming community where collaboration and enjoyment are at the forefront. The company’s flagship offering is its Rust PVE (Player vs. Environment) server, which launched in January 2021 and quickly grew in popularity during the Rust “OTV craze.” The server stands out with its blend of PVE gameplay, dedicated PVP zones, and unique Purge events at the end of each biweekly wipe.

Alliant Games is known for its innovative features, including custom plugins, skill trees, raidable bases, quality-of-life improvements, and a robust event schedule. Players can participate in a wide range of in-game activities, from massive cargo events and armored train heists to water-based adventures and special seasonal competitions.

The server also offers a tiered VIP system, allowing players to support the community while unlocking exclusive perks and benefits.

Committed to transparency and community involvement, Alliant Games encourages player feedback through Discord, hosts regular events, and maintains a responsive support team. The company also extends its presence to other multiplayer games, including a Path of Exile 2 guild, and continues to explore new ways to deliver engaging content and experiences.

Alliant Games operates under the core values of community, reliability, and innovation. Whether you’re a seasoned gamer or new to the scene, Alliant Games invites you to join its alliance and discover what makes its community unique.

For more information, visit https://alliantgames.com or contact support at support@alliant.gg

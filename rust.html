Welcome Surviors

Tired of the same old grind? We're a crew of dedicated gamers who've built a sanctuary for Rust fanatics. Forget the drama, we're here for the thrill of survival and the camaraderie of a true community. Join us and find your tribe.

Our digital gates are always open. Whether you're a seasoned veteran or a fresh spawn, our forums are buzzing with strategy, banter, and the occasional meme war. Connect with fellow players, share your epic fails, and plan your next big raid.

Getting involved is easier than crafting a sleeping bag. Jump into our Discord, say hello, and let the chaos commence. We’re always looking for legends in the making, so don't be shy. Your adventure starts now.

How We Invest in Alliant Games

Since our launch in 2020, Alliant Games has never operated for profit.

Every dollar generated through VIP & Perks sales and donations has gone directly back into covering the costs of running our servers and community.

The truth is, the community doesn’t generate enough income to fully sustain itself — the founder has personally covered the deficit to keep things running.

We rely on the generosity of our members to help keep the servers alive and the community thriving.

If you value what we’ve built together and enjoy the environment we provide, please consider supporting us by purchasing VIP or making a donation today.

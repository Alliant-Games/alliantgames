Origins

The calm before the Rift

Playstyle: PVE • Focus: Community, Creativity, Quality of Life

Welcome to Origins, the peaceful heart of the Alliant Games Rust universe.
Here, survival isn’t about constant raids or sleepless base defense — it’s about building something lasting. Whether you’re crafting a cozy homestead, trading in the marketplace, or teaming up to conquer monuments, Origins offers a timeless PVE Rust experience built for those who want challenge without chaos.

We’ve refined every detail to make Rust feel rewarding again: custom events, unique world zones, balanced economy, and quality-of-life plugins that keep gameplay smooth without breaking immersion. With no raids and dedicated community support, you can finally enjoy the creativity, exploration, and connection that Rust was meant to offer.

Find your rhythm. Build your legacy. This is where survival meets a bit of sanity — mostly.
